SAG-AFTRA

In 2023, the SAG-AFTRA strike reshaped entertainment, demanding fair wages and better conditions. It united voices, sparking a vital conversation about artists' rights and equality in the industry. This pivotal moment redefined the future of creativity and respect in entertainment.

Emboldened by unity, the aftermath ushered in a new era. Its reverberating impact rippled through entertainment, fostering an environment of fairness and inclusivity. This seismic shift continues to illuminate a path forward, empowering artists and amplifying their voices in a redefined landscape of creativity and respect
